  1. What is a segaMaker JumpStart?

          - Machine learning hub within segamaker AI => accelerates the process of training,building AI models
             it also provide pre-built Ml solutions with various domains like:NLB,computer vision.
          -these pre-built can be fine-tuned...
      use-cases:
     
         1. deploy pre-built models
         2.fine tune FMs with your data
         3.compare performance for multi-models before commiting to any approach.
     
  2.Amazon Bedrock

        -build fully-managed service 
        - provide access to FMs from amazon...
        - you can integrate them into your AWS apps
      uses:  
        1.Build AI-applications with security
        2.Build apps that generate multi-model types of text,images.
        3.Develop advanced conversational model to provide accurate responses.
